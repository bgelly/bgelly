Smart Advertising.

Rideo provides advertising partners with data and analytics to ensure every ad counts, including:

The number of unique views your ad received.

Data on the geographic location of passengers when presented with your ad, which are guaranteed to be within a radius of your business.

Tracking on customer engagement through special offers, discounts and loyalty points that are unique to Rideo's plaform.
