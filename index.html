FAQ

Drivers

How does Rideo advertising work?

The process is easy. Just attach Rideo’s tablet on the back of the passenger seat headrest to display advertisers’ content while you drive. We can provide the tablet, headrest, charger and all other gear you need if you do not have it already. Use our phone app to signal when you pick up and drop off a passenger. By displaying our tablet and driving the routes you usually drive, you can make $80 to $150 extra dollars per month, with no extra work.

Is Rideo available in my city?

Rideo is currently available in the greater New York and New Jersey metropolitan area. If you’re interested in driving with us but not working within our current operating zones, don’t worry! We’re expanding rapidly and plan to come to your city very soon.

Do I have to provide my own tablet?

Drivers can provide their own tablets or lease a tablet from Rideo. We offer a very affordable payment plan that subtracts a percentage of driver’s Rideo income over 3 months until the tablet is paid off. If drivers provide their own tablets, the tablet screen must be over 8”. Android tablets only.

Does the Rideo tablet and phone app work with Android or iOS?

Rideo’s phone app is compatible with both Android and iOS. Tablets are Android only.

How do I get paid?

Drivers are paid weekly via cash, Venmo or Paypal.

Can I earn tips on Rideo?

Yes! Rideo’s tablet allows passengers to tip their drivers through Venmo. Tips are deposited into Rideo’s account and transferred to drivers weekly in addition to the driver’s advertisement earnings.

How much can I earn driving with Rideo?

The average driver earns $90 per month driving with Rideo. Pay is based on the number of trips you make per week, and some drivers earn up to $150 in additional income. As Rideo continues to grow driver pay will increase.

Will Uber or Lyft deactivate my account for driving with Rideo?

No. Rideshare drivers are independent contractors and Uber has officially stated Rideo is not against company policy and drivers will not be penalized.

Advertisers

Why advertise with Rideo?

Rideo allows you to engage an untapped demographic of young, well-educated, and generally wealthy rideshare passengers. These passengers are economically active and ready to make an impact. All Rideo advertisements are interactive and displayed in a captive environment, making Rideo a prime vehicle for driving your business’s outreach and growth.

Is there a monthly or yearly commitment when I sign up with Rideo?

No, there is no commitment for advertisers who partner with Rideo. All of our advertising partners can cancel at the end of any month, and all of our advertisers pay month-to-month. We provide a comprehensive data & analytics report at the end of the month and stand behind the results we generate. You can decide to stick around after getting all the data on your campaign at the end of the month.

What kinds of ads can I run with Rideo?

Rideo supports personalized full-frame advertisements. All Rideo advertisements interactive and feature a live GPS map with your location pinned, along with other information. Video capabilities and more coming soon!

Can Rideo create an ad for my company?

Yes! Rideo is happy to provide advertisements for our partners. We have an experienced team of designers on hand to get your advertisement up and running. Whether you choose to provide your own advertisements or have Rideo create one for you, we’ll work with you to make sure you’re happy with the campaign you’re launching.
