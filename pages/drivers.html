Rideo was founded in 2016 in Greenpoint, Brooklyn by three friends, Bennett, Dillon and Koji, with the goal of enabling small New York City businesses to show what is great about their product or service in a unique way. We created a platform that enables our partners to reach a previously untapped audience, passengers in rideshare vehicles, while generating extra income for drivers.


Rideo is launching its first round of businesses and drivers in Spring 2017. Our first group of advertising partners will be featured on our website and receive a discounted price. Message us at info@driverideo.com or call/text (414) 238 4746 to get more information!
